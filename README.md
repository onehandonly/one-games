  ▎ A new cipher every morning. Decode the hidden quote, keep your streak alive, and share your win without spoiling the

  ▎ One quote. Hidden in code. A fresh one every day.
  ▎
  ▎ Every letter has been swapped for another. Your job: crack the cipher and reveal a quote worth reading. It starts 
  ▎ impossible and ends obvious — that little click when the words snap into place is the whole point.
  ▎
  ▎ OnePuzzle is the daily ritual for people who like their brain games calm, clever, and quick. No timers screaming at you.
  ▎  No energy bars. No pay-to-win. Just one elegant, hand-picked puzzle a day — solvable in about three minutes with your 
  ▎ morning coffee.
  ▎
  ▎ Why you'll come back tomorrow:
  ▎ • 🔠 A new cryptogram every day — one puzzle, freshly set, the same for everyone.
  ▎ • ✍️  Quotes worth decoding — wit and wisdom from voices across history, not random text.
  ▎ • 🔥 Streaks & stats — build a run you won't want to break, and watch your solve times drop.
  ▎ • 📤 Spoiler-free sharing — brag about the solve without giving the answer away.
  ▎ • 🎨 Typography-first design — clean, quiet, and a genuine pleasure to look at.
  ▎ • 📶 Plays offline — your puzzle is ready wherever you are.
  ▎
  ▎ OnePuzzle Plus (optional): go ad-free, unlock the full archive of past puzzles, add a monthly streak freeze, and pick 
  ▎ from extra themes. Free to play, forever — Plus just makes it nicer.
  ▎
  ▎ If you love Wordle, the crossword, or the classic newspaper cryptogram, this is your new daily three minutes.
  ▎
  ▎ Come decode something today.
