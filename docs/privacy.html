Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Overview

One Puzzle ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Sign in with Apple: If you choose to enable cross-device sync by signing in with your Apple ID, we will receive your name and email address (as configured in your Apple account settings). This is entirely optional and used solely to enable sync of your puzzle solve history across your devices. You can disable sync at any time in your App settings.

Information Collected Automatically

Solve History: Your puzzle solve history (including dates, times, and completion status) is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable Sign in with Apple sync, this solve history is synced to our servers to restore it on your other devices. Without sync enabled, this information never leaves your device.

No Tracking or Analytics: We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services that collect or identify you personally. We do not collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, UDID, or similar)
  • Advertising data
  • Location data
  • Browsing or usage analytics
  • Other personally identifying information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Cross-Device Sync: To sync your solve history across your devices when you enable Sign in with Apple (optional)
  • Providing the App: To operate, maintain, and improve the App and user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: By default, your solve history is stored only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Sign in with Apple sync.

Server Storage: If you enable Sign in with Apple sync, your solve history is encrypted and stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect this data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not:

  • Use third-party advertising networks
  • Collect advertising identifiers
  • Track your behavior across apps or websites
  • Sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Use analytics services that identify you personally
  • Request access to your contacts, photos, calendar, or other sensitive device data

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Apple: If you enable Sign in with Apple, your information is processed by Apple according to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request, we may disclose your information

6. Your Rights and Choices

GDPR (European Users)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we delete your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain processing of your data

CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Right to Delete: You can request that we delete your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

Other Users

You can:

  • Disable Sign in with Apple sync at any time in the App settings
  • Delete your local solve history by deleting the App and reinstalling it
  • Request deletion of your synced data by contacting us

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.
