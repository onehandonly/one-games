OnePuzzle

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 22, 2026

Overview

OnePuzzle ("the App") is published by One Hand Wonder LLC ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

Short version: The App does not track you, does not serve ads, does not collect personal information, and does not send any data to our servers.

Information We Collect

OnePuzzle does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, or contact information.
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar advertising identifiers).
  • No location data.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking.
  • No crash reporting transmitted to third-party services.

The only data the App stores is your puzzle progress, solve history, and streak counter. This data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it unless you choose to enable iCloud sync (see below).

On-Device Storage

All game state — including puzzle completions, streak counters, and settings — is saved using Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData). This data is private to the App on your device and is not accessible to us.

If you delete the App, all locally stored game data is removed with it.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you have iCloud Drive enabled in your iOS Settings, OnePuzzle may sync your game progress across your own Apple devices via iCloud. This data is managed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy and is not accessible to us.

You can disable iCloud sync for OnePuzzle at any time in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Apps Using iCloud.

No Advertising

OnePuzzle does not display advertisements and does not integrate any advertising SDKs or networks. We do not request access to the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) and have not implemented App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompts, as there is no tracking to disclose.

No Third-Party Analytics

OnePuzzle does not include any third-party analytics SDKs (such as Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or similar services). We do not collect usage statistics, session data, or behavioral telemetry.

In-App Purchases

OnePuzzle offers optional in-app purchases and/or a subscription through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment card details or any billing information. For purchase-related inquiries, please contact Apple Support.

Children's Privacy

OnePuzzle is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal data, the App is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If we materially change this privacy policy — for example by adding a feature that does collect data — we will update this page and change the effective date. We will also notify users within the App where required by law.
