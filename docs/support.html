Frequently Asked Questions

How does One Puzzle work?

One Puzzle presents you with a new logic puzzle every day. Each puzzle has a unique solution, and you have until midnight to solve it. Share your results with friends and compare your solve times!

Can I sync my progress across devices?

Yes! You can optionally enable cross-device sync by signing in with your Apple ID in the App. This will sync your solve history across all your devices. If you don't enable sync, your solve history stays on your device locally.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We don't track you, show you ads, or share your data with third parties. Your solve history stays on your device by default. Only if you enable Sign in with Apple sync will your data be stored on our servers. For complete details, please read our Privacy Policy.
