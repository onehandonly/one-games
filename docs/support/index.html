Frequently Asked Questions

How often does a new puzzle appear?
A fresh puzzle is available every day at midnight in your local time zone. Everyone around the world solves the same puzzle on the same day.

Can I play yesterday's puzzle?
The current version of OnePuzzle is designed around the daily format — only today's puzzle is available in the free tier. Past puzzles may be unlocked in a future update.

My streak reset even though I solved the puzzle. What happened?
Streaks are calculated using the device clock. If you travel across time zones or change your device's date/time settings, the streak counter may reset. We are working on improvements to make this more robust.

How do I share my result?
Tap the Share button on the result screen. This copies a text summary of your solve to the clipboard or opens the iOS Share Sheet — no image permissions required.

Does the app collect any personal data?
No. OnePuzzle does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your puzzle progress and streak are stored only on your device (or in your personal iCloud if you enable iCloud sync). See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore a purchase?
Open OnePuzzle, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Your App Store purchase history will be checked automatically.

The app won't launch / crashes on startup. What should I do?
Please try:

  • Force-quit the app and reopen it.
  • Restart your iPhone.
  • Check that your iOS version is 17.0 or later (required).
  • Delete and reinstall the app (your progress is backed up via iCloud if enabled).

If the problem persists, email us at support@onehandwonder.com with your device model and iOS version.
